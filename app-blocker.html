Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 31, 2025

At AdobixTech, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Internet Blocker Pro ("the App") operates and handles your information.

1. Information Collection and Usage

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations occur locally on your device.

What we store locally:

  • Your selected apps to block (package names only)
  • App configuration preferences

This information remains on your device and is never shared with us or third parties.

2. Accessibility Service Usage

The App requires Accessibility Service permission to:

  • Detect when apps are launched
  • Show blocking dialogs when restricted apps are opened
  • Prevent usage of blocked apps during restricted times

Important: The Accessibility Service does NOT:

  • Access any content within apps (messages, emails, etc.)
  • Record keystrokes or screen content
  • Interact with app contents beyond detecting launches
  • Collect usage statistics or personal data

3. SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Permission

This permission is used exclusively to:

  • Display blocking dialog overlays
  • Show app restriction notifications

The overlay cannot and does not interact with other app content or collect information.

4. Data Storage and Retention

The App stores all data locally on your device only:

  • Your block list (app package names only)
  • App configuration settings

Important notes:

  • No data is ever backed up to cloud services (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.)
  • No internet permission is requested - data cannot leave your device
  • All data is stored using Android's local storage mechanisms
  • Data is immediately and permanently deleted when you uninstall the App

5. Security Measures

We implement these security practices:

  • All data stored locally using Android's secure storage
  • No internet permissions - cannot transmit data
  • Accessibility Service restricted to minimum necessary functions

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
