Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 09, 2025

At AdobixTech, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Limitly: App-Block operates and handles your information with complete transparency.

1. Information Collection and Usage

The App is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • Local operation: All processing occurs exclusively on your device
  • Minimal storage: Only stores what's necessary for functionality:
    • Package names of apps you choose to block
    • Your custom blocking schedules and preferences

This information remains encrypted on your device and is never accessible to us or any third parties.

4. PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS Permission

This permission is used strictly for:

  • Monitoring app usage time to enforce time-based restrictions
  • Providing accurate usage statistics for your blocked apps

Privacy Guarantees:

  • No personal data collection: Only tracks which apps are used and for how long
  • Local processing only: All usage data remains on your device
  • No cross-app tracking: Cannot see what you do within any app
  • No network transmission: Usage statistics never leave your device

2. Accessibility Service Usage

The App requires Accessibility Service permission solely for these technical functions:

  • Detect when apps are launched (app identification only)
  • Prevent usage of blocked apps when they are opened

Important Limitations:

  • No content access: Cannot and does not read messages, emails, or any in-app content
  • No tracking: Never records keystrokes, screenshots, or browsing activity
  • No data collection: Does not gather usage statistics or personal information
  • No network access: All processing occurs 100% on-device

3. SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Permission

This permission is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying the blocking interface when restricted apps are launched

Security Assurance: The overlay is used only to enforce app restrictions and cannot interact with other apps or extract any information.

4. Data Storage and Retention

The App maintains strict data handling practices:

  • Storage method: Android's encrypted local storage
  • Data types stored:
    • Your blocklist (app package names only)
  • Automatic deletion: All data is permanently erased upon uninstallation
  • No backups: Never synced to cloud services (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.)
  • No data transfer: Information never leaves your device

5. Security Measures

We implement multiple protection layers:

  • Android's built-in sandbox security for all stored data
  • No internet permission in manifest (technical enforcement)
  • Accessibility Service restricted to package name detection only
  • Regular security audits of all permission usage

6. Children's Privacy

The App complies with COPPA regulations:

  • Not designed for children under 18
  • No age-based data collection differences
  • Parental control features contain no data gathering

7. Policy Updates

We will:

  • Notify users of material changes via in-app alerts
  • Maintain version history with change dates
  • Never reduce your privacy protections without consent
