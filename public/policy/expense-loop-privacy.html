Expense Loop – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Expense Loop is developed by AdobixTech. The app stores the financial entries you create so you can track spending patterns and manage personal budgets. It does not connect directly to your bank accounts or cards.

Information the App Stores

  • Income and expense entries you manually add
  • Categories, budget values, and optional notes
  • Dates, totals, and usage summaries generated by the app

What Expense Loop does not collect

  • No bank credentials, debit/credit card details, or payment passwords
  • No contacts, messages, photos, or call logs
  • No personally identifiable profile data unless you explicitly type it into notes

How We Use This Information

  • To show transaction history and category-based summaries
  • To help you monitor budgets and spending trends
  • To improve app stability and user experience over time

We do not sell your data or share your personal finance entries with third parties.

Storage and Permissions

  • Your core app data is stored locally on your device.
  • If your device backup is enabled, data may be included in your own backup system.
  • Expense Loop requests only required permissions for core functionality.

Your Choices

  • Edit and remove entries at any time inside the app
  • Clear app data from device settings if needed
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data

Children's Privacy

Expense Loop is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page. Continued use of Expense Loop indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
