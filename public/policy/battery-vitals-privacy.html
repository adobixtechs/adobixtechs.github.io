Battery Vitals – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Battery Vitals ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how Adobix Tech collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application, Battery Vitals (the "App").

We are committed to transparency and honesty, which is the core principle of Battery Vitals.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve the App, we collect the following information:

A. Information Collected Automatically (For Core App Functionality)

  • Battery & Device Data: We collect information about your device's battery, including its health status (for example, temperature, voltage, and capacity), and charging status. This is the core functionality of the App.
  • Installed Application Data: To provide the "App Usage" and "App Vitals" features, our App needs to identify which other apps are running and how much battery they are consuming. This information is processed locally on your device to show you usage statistics. This data is not uploaded to our servers.

B. Information Collected for Analytics & Ads

  • Analytics Data: We use analytics services (for example, Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand how users interact with our App. This helps us diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve features. This may include device model, OS version, and in-app activity.
  • Advertising Data (Free Version Only): The free version of Battery Vitals is supported by ads. To display relevant ads, we work with ad partners (for example, Google AdMob) who may collect your device's Advertising ID.
  • Pro Version: This advertising data is not collected if you purchase Battery Vitals Pro.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core Features: To display battery health, monitor app usage, and (in the Pro version) provide Smart Alerts.
  • To Improve Our App: To analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and improve Battery Vitals.
  • To Display Ads (Free Version): To support development by showing non-personalized or personalized ads in the free version.
  • To Fulfill Your Pro Purchase: To verify your purchase and unlock the ad-free, premium version of the App.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with third-party services to make our App function.

Our App may include the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: Used for processing in-app purchases ("Pro" version) and core Android functions.
  • Google AdMob: Ad partner for the free version. Their privacy policy governs the data they collect. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Analytics service for improving app quality and reliability. Google Privacy Policy

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reading them to understand how they handle your data.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data and use commercially reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

A key part of our security is that sensitive data, such as your list of installed apps and their usage, is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

5. Children's Privacy

Battery Vitals is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page and revising the "Last Updated" date.
