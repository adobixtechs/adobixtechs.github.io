EncryptedQR Pro – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to EncryptedQR Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

What You Provide

  • QR Code Content: Text, URLs, or data you input to generate QR codes. This is processed locally on your device only.
  • Passwords: If you encrypt QR codes, passwords are stored locally on your device only.

What We Collect Automatically

  • App Settings: Your preferences (theme, font, vibration, sound settings) stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device info needed for app functionality.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Payment information
  • QR code content on our servers (all content is processed locally only)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide QR code generation, scanning, and encryption features
  • To remember your app preferences
  • To maintain and improve the app

Data Storage and Security

  • All QR code data and passwords are stored locally on your device only.
  • We do not have servers that store your QR code content.
  • Encryption happens locally on your device.
  • Your data is never transmitted to our servers.

Important: All QR code data is processed locally on your device. We do not have access to your QR code content or passwords.

Permissions

Camera

Used only to scan QR codes. Camera feed is not recorded or stored.

Storage / Photos

Used only to save QR code images you generate. We do not read your existing photos.

Vibration

Used for haptic feedback when scanning (optional and can be disabled).

Third‑Party Services

  • Google Fonts: Used for font options. No personal information is transmitted by us.
  • App Stores: Payment processing is handled by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not see your payment information.
  • Firebase: May collect anonymous usage data and crash reports. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal information. We may share data only:

  • If required by law or legal process
  • With trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements to help us operate and improve the app

Your Rights

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Reset app preferences through settings
  • Revoke permissions (camera, storage, vibration) through device settings
  • Disable features like vibration and sound

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it where possible.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Data Retention

  • QR code content: not stored by us; processed locally
  • App preferences: stored locally until you delete the app or clear data
  • QR code images: stored in your photo gallery, under your control
