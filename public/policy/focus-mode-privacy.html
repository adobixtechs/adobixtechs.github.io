Focus Mode – Privacy Policy

Focus Mode is a timer app that helps you work in focused sessions with breaks.

This policy explains how Focus Mode handles your data.

Introduction

Focus Mode is developed by AdobixTech. We collect and store only the information needed to run focus sessions and show you simple statistics. We do not use your data for advertising or selling to others.

Information Stored on Your Device

  • Timer settings such as focus length and break length
  • History of completed focus sessions (for example, number of sessions and duration)
  • Notification preferences for session start and end

What Focus Mode does not collect

  • No content of what you are working on
  • No files, documents, or notes
  • No personal contacts, messages, or photos

How We Use This Information

We use the data stored on your device only to:

  • Run accurate timers for focus and break periods
  • Show basic statistics, such as how many sessions you completed
  • Send local notifications when a session ends or a break finishes

Permissions

Focus Mode may request these permissions:

  • Notifications – to inform you when focus or break sessions start and end
  • Foreground service – to keep the timer running reliably even when the app is in the background

You can turn these permissions off at any time in your device settings, but timers or alerts may stop working correctly.

Storage and Sharing

  • Your session history and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • If your device uses system backups, this data may be included in those backups.
  • We may receive anonymous crash reports or diagnostics to improve stability.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information.

Your Choices

  • Change or reset your timer settings at any time
  • Clear app data from system settings to remove stored history
  • Uninstall Focus Mode to delete the app and its data from your device

Children’s Privacy

Focus Mode is not aimed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information about children. If you believe a child has used the app and provided personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update this page. By continuing to use Focus Mode after updates, you agree to the revised policy.
