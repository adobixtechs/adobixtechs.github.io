Expense – Privacy Policy

Expense is a simple, everyday expense tracker.

This policy explains how Expense handles the data you enter.

Introduction

Expense is developed by AdobixTech. The app stores the expense entries you type so you can see how you spend money day to day. It does not connect to bank accounts or payment cards.

Information the App Stores

  • Amounts you enter for each expense
  • Categories you choose (for example, food, travel, bills)
  • Dates of expenses and optional short notes

What Expense does not collect

  • No bank logins or card numbers
  • No contacts, photos, messages, or precise location
  • No personal identification like name or email, unless you type it into a note yourself

How We Use This Information

Your entries are used only to:

  • List your expenses in the app
  • Show simple totals and summaries by date or category

We do not use this data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Storage and Permissions

  • Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • If your device uses cloud or system backups, the data may be included in those backups.
  • Expense does not normally need sensitive permissions beyond basic storage used by the app.

Your Choices

  • Edit or delete individual expenses at any time
  • Clear app data from system settings to remove all entries
  • Uninstall Expense to remove the app and stored data from your device

Children’s Privacy

Expense is primarily intended for adults managing personal finances and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted here. By continuing to use Expense after updates, you accept the revised policy.
