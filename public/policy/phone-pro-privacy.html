Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AdobixTech ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Phone Pro – Call Manager mobile application ("App"). Phone Pro – Call Manager is developed by AdobixTech. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1. Information the App Uses

1.1 Contact Information

The App accesses your device's contact list to provide dialer and contact management. This may include:

  • Contact names and phone numbers
  • Contact photos (if stored in your contacts)
  • Email addresses and other contact details
  • Company, job title, and notes

1.2 Call Information

To provide call and call‑history features, the App uses:

  • Call history (incoming, outgoing, missed)
  • Phone numbers and call duration
  • Timestamps and phone state (for example, to show incoming calls)

1.3 In‑Call Voice Visualization

During a call, the App may use the microphone (with your permission) only to display a voice‑level visualization on the call screen. This is processed in real time on your device only; no audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

2. How We Use This Information

We use the above information solely to:

  • Provide dialer, contact management, and call history
  • Display and manage incoming/outgoing calls and the in‑call screen
  • Support T9 search, favorites, and contact organization
  • Show reminders and notifications you set (for example, call reminders)
  • Improve your experience within the App

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

  • Local only: Contact and call data stays on your device. We do not upload it to our servers or third parties.
  • No analytics: We do not use third‑party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. We do not collect usage statistics or crash reports from the App.
  • No selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4. Permissions We Request

The App may request these permissions so features work correctly:

  • READ_CONTACTS – To display and manage your contact list
  • WRITE_CONTACTS – To add, edit, or delete contacts
  • CALL_PHONE – To initiate phone calls from the App
  • READ_CALL_LOG – To display call history and recent calls
  • READ_PHONE_STATE – To detect incoming calls and show the in‑call screen
  • MANAGE_OWN_CALLS – To manage your calls (for example, answer, reject, hold)
  • RECORD_AUDIO – For in‑call voice‑level visualization only (no recording or storage)
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To show call and reminder notifications
  • BLUETOOTH / BLUETOOTH_CONNECT – To use Bluetooth devices during calls
  • VIBRATE – For vibration on incoming calls and alerts
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE / FOREGROUND_SERVICE_PHONE_CALL – To display and control ongoing calls
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM – To trigger reminder notifications at the set time

We do not request Camera or SMS permissions. "Send message" in the App only opens your default messaging app; we do not read or send SMS.

5. Data Retention

Contact and call data remains on your device for as long as the App is installed and you do not delete it. You can remove data by:

  • Uninstalling the App
  • Clearing the App's data in your device settings
  • Deleting or editing contacts and call history within the App where supported

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and correct your contacts and call data on your device
  • Withdraw permissions in your device settings (some features may stop working)
  • Delete data by clearing App data or uninstalling the App

If you are in a region with extra data rights (for example, GDPR), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us using the details below to exercise them.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy. We recommend checking this page periodically.
