Limitly: App Blocker – Privacy Policy

Limitly helps you take control of your digital habits by blocking distracting apps, setting schedules, and boosting productivity.

Effective Date: June 4, 2025

Introduction

At AdobixTech, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Limitly: App Blocker operates and handles your information with complete transparency. By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection and Usage

The app is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle:

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.
  • Local operation: All processing occurs exclusively on your device.
  • Minimal storage: Only stores what is necessary for functionality:
    • Package names of apps you choose to block
    • Your custom blocking schedules and preferences

This information remains on your device and is never accessible to us or any third parties.

2. PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS Permission

This permission is used strictly to:

  • Monitor app usage time to enforce time‑based restrictions
  • Provide accurate usage statistics for your blocked apps

Privacy guarantees:

  • No personal data collection: Only tracks which apps are used and for how long.
  • Local processing only: All usage data remains on your device.
  • No cross‑app tracking: We cannot see what you do within any app.
  • No network transmission: Usage statistics never leave your device.

3. Accessibility Service Usage

The app requires Accessibility Service permission solely for these technical functions:

  • Detect when apps are launched (app identification only).
  • Prevent usage of blocked apps when they are opened.

Important limitations:

  • No content access: We cannot and do not read messages, emails, or any in‑app content.
  • No tracking: We never record keystrokes, screenshots, or browsing activity.
  • No data collection: We do not gather usage statistics or personal information for analytics.
  • No network access: All processing occurs 100% on‑device.

4. Billing Permission

The com.android.vending.BILLING permission is used exclusively for:

  • Processing in‑app purchases and subscriptions through Google Play's secure billing system.
  • Verifying purchase status to unlock premium features.

Privacy assurance:

  • No payment details access: All transactions are processed by Google Play; we never see or store your payment information.
  • Minimal data: We only receive purchase confirmation tokens from Google.
  • Local validation: Purchase verification occurs on‑device when possible.
  • No personal data linkage: Purchase records are not associated with identifiable personal information by us.

5. SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Permission

This permission is used exclusively to:

  • Display the blocking interface when restricted apps are launched.

Security assurance: The overlay is used only to enforce app restrictions and cannot extract information from other apps.

6. Data Storage and Retention

The app maintains strict data handling practices:

  • Storage method: Android's local storage on your device.
  • Data types stored:
    • Your blocklist (app package names only).
    • Your blocking schedules and configuration preferences.
  • Automatic deletion: All app data is permanently erased upon uninstallation.
  • No cloud backups by us: We do not sync your data to any external servers. System‑level backups are controlled by your device settings.
  • No data transfer: This information never leaves your device through the app.

7. Security Measures

We implement multiple protection layers:

  • Relying on Android's sandbox security for all stored data.
  • Not requesting unnecessary permissions (for example, contacts, SMS, or location).
  • Using Accessibility and usage permissions only for their documented purposes.
  • Regular internal reviews of permission usage and app behavior.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app is not designed for children under 18 and does not target them. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used Limitly and provided personal information, please contact us and we will assist with removal where possible.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the “Last Updated” date associated with the policy.
  • Notify users of material changes via in‑app messages where appropriate.

Continued use of Limitly after such changes means you accept the updated policy.
