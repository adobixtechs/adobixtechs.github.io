Internet Blocker Pro – Privacy Policy

Internet Blocker Pro helps you block distracting internet usage and stay focused by controlling access to online content.

Effective date: April 28, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Internet Blocker Pro! Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information. Internet Blocker Pro is designed so that all blocking rules and preferences remain on your device.

No Data Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This includes:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.).
  • Device identifiers (such as IMEI, MAC address).
  • Browsing history or detailed app usage logs.

All blocking rules and configuration are stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Permissions Used

The app may request certain permissions so that blocking can work correctly:

  • Usage Access: To detect which apps are running when you use app‑level blocking.
  • Device admin or similar control permission (where applicable): To help enforce blocking rules reliably.
  • Network / VPN‑style control (for some variants): To block network access for selected apps or connections, where supported.

Internet Blocker Pro itself does not request general internet access for tracking or analytics. Permissions are used only to apply the blocking rules you configure.

No Third‑Party Sharing

Because we do not collect data, there is no information to share with advertisers, analytics services, or any other third parties. All decisions about what to block and how long to block it are kept on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Internet Blocker Pro complies with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA because we do not collect data from users of any age. The app can be used as a focus and well‑being tool, but we do not collect or process personal information from children.

Your Control

  • You can modify or disable blocking at any time from within the app.
  • All settings, blocklists, and schedules are stored only on your device.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data related to Internet Blocker Pro.
  • You can revoke permissions such as usage access or device admin in your system settings at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of Internet Blocker Pro after changes means you accept the updated policy.
