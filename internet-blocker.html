Privacy Policy

Effective date: 28 April 2025

Welcome to Internet Blocker Pro! Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

No Data Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Browsing history or app usage logs

All blocking rules are stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Permissions Used

The app requires these permissions to function:

  • Usage Access - To detect which apps are running for blocking
  • Device Admin Permission - To enforce app blocking effectively
  • No Internet Permission - The app doesn't connect to any external servers

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we don't collect any data, nothing is shared with advertisers, analytics services, or other third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) as we don't collect any data from users of any age.

Your Control

  • You can modify or disable blocking at any time
  • All settings are stored only on your device
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page.
