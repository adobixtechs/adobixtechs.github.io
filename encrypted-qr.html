  • QR Code Content: When you generate QR codes, you may input text, URLs, or other data. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)

  • Contact lists or address books
