Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 February 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using Expenses Flow.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information when you use Google Authentication and Google Drive Sync:

  • Google Account Information: Your email and profile name for authentication purposes.
  • Stored Data: Expense tracking and budgeting data that you choose to sync with Google Drive.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Google Authentication is used to allow secure sign-in to your account.
  • Google Drive Sync is used to store and restore your financial data securely.
  • All stored data remains private and accessible only by you.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) through Google Drive Sync. We do not have access to your data, and it is not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google services for authentication and cloud storage. Google’s privacy policy can be reviewed here.

5. Your Choices & Control

  • You can disconnect Google Authentication at any time in the app settings.
  • You can delete your synced data from Google Drive manually or via the app settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at adobixtech@gmail.com.
