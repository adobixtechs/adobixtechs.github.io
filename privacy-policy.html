Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 February 2025

Welcome to Expenses Flow! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We only collect minimal user information necessary for app functionality:

  • Google Login: We authenticate users via Google Login but do not store any personal information on our servers.
  • Google Drive Access: The app requests permission to store data directly in your Google Drive. We do not access, store, or manage this data on our servers.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data for app improvements. This data does not contain personally identifiable information.
  • Notifications: We request notification permissions to send important updates and reminders related to the app.

How We Use Your Data

  • Google Authentication is used to allow secure sign-in to your account.
  • Google Drive Sync is used to store and restore your financial data securely.
  • All stored data remains private and accessible only by you.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) through Google Drive Sync. We do not have access to your data, and it is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use Google services for authentication and cloud storage. Google's privacy policy can be reviewed here.

Your Choices & Control

  • You can disconnect Google Authentication at any time in the app settings.
  • You can delete your synced data from Google Drive manually or via the app settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
