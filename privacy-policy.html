Privacy Policy for Expenses Flow

Effective Date: 21 Nov 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Expenses Flow, an application developed by Adobix Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our app. By using Expenses Flow, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We collect and store your Google account email for authentication.
  • Financial Data: Your expenses, income, and custom categories are stored locally and optionally backed up to Google Drive.
  • Device Information: Non-personal data such as device type and operating system may be collected. Firebase Analytics is used to analyze app usage.

Data Usage

All user financial data is stored locally and, if enabled, backed up to Google Drive. We do not collect, share, or sell financial data. Users have full control to add, edit, delete, and update their data.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Your data remains on your device.
  • Google Drive Backup: Users can sync data to their personal Google Drive.
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.

Permissions Used

  • Internet Access: Required for authentication and syncing with Google Drive.
  • Biometric Authentication: Enhances security for login.
  • Receive Boot Completed: Required to resume tasks after a device reboot.
  • Google Drive Access: Needed for optional cloud backups.
