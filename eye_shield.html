Privacy Policy

Eye Shield - Smart Screen Dimmer

1. Introduction

Welcome to Eye Shield - Smart Screen Dimmer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Eye Shield, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Eye Shield is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • App Preferences: Your screen dimming settings, filter preferences, and scheduling preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Device Information: Basic device information necessary for app functionality (Android version, screen resolution)
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Browsing history
  • Financial information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide screen dimming and blue light filtering features
  • Personalization: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Performance: To optimize app performance and fix technical issues
  • Improvements: To enhance user experience and add new features
  • Notifications: To provide essential app notifications and service status updates

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local data encryption
  • Secure data transmission (if any)
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication

5. Permissions We Request

Eye Shield requires the following Android permissions to function properly:

5.1 Essential Permissions

  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: Required to display the screen overlay for dimming and filtering
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Required to run the overlay service in the background
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required to show service status notifications (Android 13+)

5.2 Optional Permissions

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: For auto-scheduling features (optional)
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM: For precise alarm scheduling (optional)
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To restore scheduled settings after device restart (optional)
