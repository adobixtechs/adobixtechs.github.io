Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Expenses Flow?
Simply download the app from the Google Play Store, create an account, and start tracking your expenses. The app will guide you through the initial setup process.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes, your data is stored securely on your device and in your linked cloud drive. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information.
Can I sync my data across devices?
Yes, you can sync your data across multiple devices by linking your Google Drive account. This ensures your data is always up to date.
How do I set up a budget?
Go to the Budget section, click "Create New Budget", and follow the prompts to set your monthly or category-specific budgets.
What payment methods can I track?
You can track cash, credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and any other payment methods. The app allows you to customize payment categories.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data. You can choose to export your data in various formats including CSV and PDF.
How can I contact support?
You can reach our support team at support@expensesflow.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
