Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expenses Flow?
Expenses Flow is a comprehensive expense tracking and budgeting app designed to help you manage your finances effectively. It allows you to track daily expenses, set budgets, and analyze your spending patterns.
How do I get started with Expenses Flow?
Getting started is easy! Simply download the app from the Google Play Store, sign in with your Google account, and you can begin tracking your expenses right away. The app will guide you through the initial setup process.
Is my data secure?
Yes, your data security is our top priority. All your financial data is stored locally on your device and can be backed up to your linked cloud drive. We don't store any sensitive information on our servers.
Can I set monthly budgets?
Absolutely! You can set monthly budgets for different categories, track your spending against these budgets, and receive notifications when you're close to exceeding them.
How do I add expenses?
Adding expenses is simple. Just tap the "+" button, enter the amount, select a category, add any notes, and save. You can also add recurring expenses for regular payments.
What categories are available?
The app comes with predefined categories like Food, Transport, Shopping, Bills, Entertainment, and more. You can also create custom categories to better suit your needs.
How do I set up cloud backup?
To set up cloud backup, go to Settings > Backup & Restore, and link your Google Drive account. The app will automatically backup your data periodically.
What payment methods are supported?
The app supports all major payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. You can add custom payment methods as well.
How do I get support?
For support, you can email us at adobixtech@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, Expenses Flow is completely free to use with all basic features. We offer a premium version with additional features for users who need more advanced functionality.
Can I use the app offline?
Yes, the app works perfectly offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. When you're back online, it will sync with your cloud backup.
How do I reset my password?
Since we use Google Sign-in, you don't need to remember a password. If you need to change your Google account password, you can do so through your Google account settings.
What devices are supported?
Expenses Flow is available for Android devices running Android 6.0 (Marshmallow) or higher. We're working on an iOS version that will be available soon.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data. Please note that this action cannot be undone.
