Frequently Asked Questions

What is Expenses Flow?
Expenses Flow is a smart expense and budget tracking app that helps you manage your finances effortlessly. You can track your income and expenses, set budgets, and analyze spending habits—all while keeping your data private and secure.
Is there a mobile application available?
Yes! Expenses Flow is available as a mobile app, allowing you to track your finances anytime, anywhere. Manage your expenses, set budgets, and gain insights right from your smartphone.
How is my financial data stored?
Your financial data is only stored on your device or your linked cloud drive (such as Google Drive or iCloud). We do not store any data on external servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Can I set budgets and track my expenses?
Absolutely! You can create customized budgets for different categories, set spending limits, and track your expenses to stay in control of your finances.
Does Expenses Flow require an internet connection?
No! You can use Expenses Flow offline, and all your data stays on your device. If you choose to back up your data to the cloud, an internet connection will be required for syncing.
Is my data secure?
Yes! Your data is completely private because it is only stored on your device or your personal cloud drive. We do not have access to your financial information, and there is no third-party tracking.
Can I see reports and insights on my spending?
Yes! Expenses Flow provides detailed reports, charts, and insights to help you understand your spending habits, track trends, and make informed financial decisions.
Is Expenses Flow free to use?
Expenses Flow offers both free and premium features. The free version provides essential expense tracking, while premium features may include advanced budgeting, custom reports, and more.
How do I back up my data?
You can back up your data by linking your Google Drive or iCloud account. This ensures your financial information is safely stored and can be restored when needed.
How do I get started with Expenses Flow?
Simply download the app, set up your categories, and start tracking your expenses. It’s that easy! Got more questions? Reach out to us anytime!
