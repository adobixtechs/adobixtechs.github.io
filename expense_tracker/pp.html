Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Effective Date: 21 Nov 2024

Introduction

Privacy Policy for the Expense Tracker app developed by Adobixtech. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information. By using Expense Tracker, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

The Expense Tracker app requires users to log in using their Google email for authentication. Apart from the email address used for login, we do not collect any other personal data.

Data Usage

All user data, including expenses, income, and custom categories, is stored locally on the user's device. We do not collect, share, or store this data on any server or external storage. Users have full control over their data, with the ability to add, edit, delete, and update their expenses and income.

Custom Expense Categories

Users can create and manage custom expense categories within the app. This data is also stored locally on the device and is not transmitted or shared with any third party.

Children's Privacy

There are no age restrictions for using the Expense Tracker app. However, we encourage parents and guardians to guide younger users in understanding personal finance management.

Third-Party Services

The Expense Tracker app does not use third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertisements. Your data is secure and remains on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on our website or within the app, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

Contact Us
